Our Story

Bistro Gheorgheni is a family-owned restaurant, driven by a passion for the rich traditions of Transylvanian cuisine. Over the years, we have blended old-world recipes with modern techniques to create a dining experience that is both comforting and inspiring. We believe that every meal should be memorable, and every guest should feel at home.
